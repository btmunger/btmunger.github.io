5. Amancio Ortega

Amancio Ortega is an 83 year old Spanish Billionaire who claims the title of the richest retailer in the world. He is the cofounder of Inditex, the biggest fashion group in the entire world. In a typical year, Ortega earns around 400 million dollars a year.
