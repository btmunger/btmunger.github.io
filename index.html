The Five Richest People in The World

By Brian Munger

5. Amancio Ortega

Amancio Ortega is an 83 year old Spanish Billionaire who claims the title of the richest retailer in the world. He is the cofounder of Inditex, the biggest fashion group in the entire world. In a typical year, Ortega earns around 400 million dollars a year. In his personal life, Ortega is known to be very private and lives a quite simple life. In his younger years, he was the youngest of four children, and dropped out of school at the age of fourteen to move with his dad who was a railroad worker. Ortega is now currently retired and lives with his wife and four children in Coruna, Spain.

4. Warren Buffett

Warren Buffett is an American inventor and speaker, most famous for being the CEO of Berkshire Hathaway: a multinational conglomerate holding company. At the age of 88, he has written nine books about the art of investing and other topics which got him to the powerful place that he is in now.

3. Bernard Arnault

Bernard Arnault is an 70 year old French investor, entrepreneur, and art collector. Arnault holds the important and powerful position of Chairman and Chief Executive of Louis Vuitton, a very popular luxury retail company. Currently, Arnault holds the title of the richest person in the entire continent of Europe!

2. Bill Gates

Bill Gates is perhaps one of the most recognizable billionaires ever born. Gates is best known for his positon as founder of the tech giant Microsoft, a company which develops software, such as Windows, and computers.

1. Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos currently holds the spot as the richest person in the world. Even after his recent divorce which cost him an estimated 70 billion dollars, Bezos is thriving at the top of the list. Bezos is the founder and CEO of the incredibly successful online shopping site Amazon.
